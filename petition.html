Stop U.S. Interference in Alberta

← Back to Campaign

This petition was launched by Chelsea Barnowich, a Calgary-based activist who has become one of the most effective grassroots organizers in the fight against UCP overreach. Chelsea previously spearheaded the Recall Tanya Fir citizen initiative in Calgary-Peigan, demonstrating exactly the kind of relentless, on-the-ground accountability work that the UCP hopes Albertans are too tired to sustain. This goes to show that they are not.

Her latest effort calls on the House of Commons to invoke Section 53 of the Supreme Court Act to refer the constitutionality of Alberta's Justice Statutes Amendment Act, 2025 to the Supreme Court of Canada. The petition also calls on the Foreign Influence Transparency Commissioner to immediately investigate the meetings between the Alberta Prosperity Project and the Trump administration.

Please sign this petition to make your voice heard at the federal level on the UCP separatist agenda.

This petition is open to all Canadians
